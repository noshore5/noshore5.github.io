Wavelet Coherence Analysis

Torrence, C. & Webster, P.J.
Journal of Climate, 1999

A comprehensive introduction to wavelet coherence analysis, providing the theoretical foundation for understanding relationships between time series in the time-frequency domain.

This seminal paper introduces the concept of wavelet coherence as a localized correlation coefficient in time-frequency space, allowing for the identification of regions in time-frequency space where two time series co-vary.

Financial Signal Processing

Gencay, R., Selcuk, F. & Whitcher, B.
Academic Press, 2001

An introduction to wavelets and other filtering methods in finance, covering practical applications of wavelet analysis in financial time series.

This comprehensive book covers the application of wavelet transforms to financial data analysis, including denoising, feature extraction, and risk management applications.

S. Chudoba, M. Kostur, et al.
SoftwareX, 2020

The Fast Continuous Wavelet Transform (FCWT) is an efficient algorithm for computing the CWT, enabling real-time and large-scale time-frequency analysis. The FCWT library is open source and widely used in scientific computing.
